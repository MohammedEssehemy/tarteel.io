Salaam!

Welcome to the Tarteel 10,000 Challenge! Thank you for helping us build the world's first public, open-source dataset of Quran recitations by ordinary people, like you and me.

How it works

This app will provide you with 5 random verses to recite. Once given the ayah, you will record your recitation of it. These recitations will be released as an open-source initiative to encourage machine learning applications based on recitations of the Quran.
